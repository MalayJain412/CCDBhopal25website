Submission Guidelines

Please review these guidelines before submitting your proposal.

Session Format

  • • Technical Talks: 30-45 minutes including Q&A
  • • Workshops: 60-90 minutes hands-on sessions
  • • Lightning Talks: 10-15 minutes quick presentations
  • • Panel Discussions: 45-60 minutes moderated discussions

Submission Requirements

  • • Detailed session abstract (150-300 words)
  • • Speaker bio and high-resolution photo
  • • Outline of key takeaways for attendees
  • • Links to previous talks or relevant work
