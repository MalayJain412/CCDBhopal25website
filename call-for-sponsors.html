Sponsorship Policy

  1. Sponsorship prices listed do not include taxes. GST and any other applicable fees will be charged on top of the list prices.
  2. Full payment for the chosen sponsorship package must be made before the event. Payment instructions and deadlines will be provided upon confirmation.
  3. We respect the privacy of our participants. We do not share attendee data with sponsors. Sponsors may interact with participants at the event venue and through their branding presence.
  4. All sponsorships are subject to approval by the organizing team. We reserve the right to decline sponsorships that do not align with our event values.
