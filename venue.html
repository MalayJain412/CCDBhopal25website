Minto Hall (Kushabhau Thakre Hall) is a blend of colonial grandeur and modern comfort, built in 1909 for Lord Minto and later serving as the Old Vidhan Sabha. Its majestic architecture, lush lawns, and panoramic Upper Lake views make it a must-visit for anyone coming to Bhopal.
